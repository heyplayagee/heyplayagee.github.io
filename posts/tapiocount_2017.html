Tapiocount 2017
Posted December 25, 2017

In February of 2017, I decided to keep track of my tapioca/boba/bubble tea/bubbles intake.
Thanks Ran D. for the inspiration idea to totally copy.

Fun stats, quick maths

  • Days with more than one tapioca drink: 4
  • Most visited tapioca chain: i-Tea (16 times!)
  • Most visited tapioca location: i-Tea Sunset (10)
  • Most ordered drinks:
    1. Black milk tea
    2. Earl grey milk tea
    3. Jasmine milk tea
  • Month with the most tapioca: July and September tied at 13!
  • Week with the most tapioca (Sun-Sat): September 17-23 and November 19-25 with 5 tapioca each
  • Most days in a row getting tapioca: 4 (September 15-18 and September 21-24)
  • Day of the week with the most tapioca consumed: Sunday (23) with Saturday (21) close behind
  • Day of the week with the least tapioca consumed: Wednesday (5)
  • Last tapioca being 23: August 16 (5 days before)
  • First tapioca being 24: August 25 (4 days after)
